Secrets to happiness:

Happiness as we know is a very vital ingredients to living a good life. It can be internally, externally, or both. While many limit happiness to materials things, status, popularity, degrees, and many others feel we can't be happy in this life as this world was not created for it. Well, happiness can be gotten from this life, however, complete happiness can't be gotten because we'll always have challenges at some point or the other. This life was created to be a mix of good and bad, While the next life was created to be either perfectly good or bad. Those fortunate enough to be admitted into garden of bliss will know no pain, not even a slight headache, rather it will peace upon peace, unlike this world where there's a chance of things becoming bad, over there everything will be good for the fortunate ones. While for those on the flip side, it will be all bad not even a break from the situation, it'll be the same for them forever.Some of the keys to happiness are listed below:

  • Strong faith in ALLAH(SWT)
  • Tawbah i.e repentance
  • Shukr i.e gratitude
